John D. Storey

Principal Investigator

John Storey is a professor of statistics and genomics at Princeton University, where he is officially the William R. Harman ’63 and Mary-Love Harman Professor in Genomics. John’s research has been concerned with developing and applying statistical methods to high-dimensional data in genomics. In addition to being primarily appointed in the Lewis-Sigler Institute for Integrative Genomics, he is an associated faculty member in five other departments or insitutes on campus.
