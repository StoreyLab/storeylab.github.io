Function digraphs of quadratic maps modulo p

Christie L Gilbert, Joseph D Kolesar, Clifford A Reiter, John D Storey

Abstract

In this paper we consider geometric representations of the iteration of quadratic polynomials modulo p. This is a discrete analogue of the classical quadratic Julia sets which have been the subject of much study.

Publication
Fibonacci Quarterly, 39 (1): 32–49
